Mission, vision & values

To advance the status and rights of women and girls, and to promote peace, security, and socio-economic justice from a feminist perspective through dissemination of knowledge, advancing legislation, research, and public events.

Our Mission

A society where all women enjoy equal rights in all fields. A society without gender-based violence. A society in which women’s voices are heard. A society in which women have full and equal access.

Our Vision

1) Empowerment: Isha L'Isha believes that women are powerful and capable individuals.

2) Solidarity: Isha L'Isha believes that women are stronger together.

3) Justice: Isha L'Isha believes that all women deserve to be treated with dignity and respect.

4) Diversity: Isha L'Isha believes that women come from all walks of life.
